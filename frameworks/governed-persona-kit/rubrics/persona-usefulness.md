# Persona Usefulness Rubric

Evaluate how effectively personas contribute to decision quality and organizational outcomes.

## Evaluation Criteria

### 1. Decision Quality Impact (30%)
Measures how much personas improve the quality of decisions.

**Exemplary (4 points):**
- Consistently identifies critical factors missed by human evaluation
- Provides evidence-based insights that change decision outcomes
- Reduces decision errors by >40% compared to baseline
- Enables faster, more confident decision making

**Proficient (3 points):**
- Regularly contributes relevant perspectives to decisions
- Provides evidence that supports or challenges assumptions
- Reduces decision errors by 20-40% compared to baseline
- Improves decision confidence and speed

**Developing (2 points):**
- Occasionally provides useful perspectives
- Some evidence-based contributions to decisions
- Reduces decision errors by 10-20% compared to baseline
- Modest improvement in decision quality

**Basic (1 point):**
- Rarely contributes meaningful insights
- Limited evidence-based reasoning
- Minimal impact on decision quality (<10% improvement)
- Often redundant with human evaluation

**Unacceptable (0 points):**
- No measurable impact on decision quality
- Hinders rather than helps decision making
- Increases decision time or complexity without benefit

### 2. Evidence Quality (25%)
Assesses the strength and relevance of evidence cited by personas.

**Exemplary (4 points):**
- Consistently cites strong, verifiable evidence sources
- Evidence directly supports claims and recommendations
- Diverse evidence types (metrics, research, case studies)
- Evidence currency and relevance consistently high

**Proficient (3 points):**
- Regularly cites appropriate evidence sources
- Most claims well-supported by evidence
- Good mix of evidence types and sources
- Evidence generally current and relevant

**Developing (2 points):**
- Some evidence citation but inconsistent
- Claims occasionally unsupported or weakly supported
- Limited variety in evidence types
- Some evidence outdated or marginally relevant

**Basic (1 point):**
- Minimal evidence citation
- Many claims lack supporting evidence
- Reliance on weak or inappropriate sources
- Evidence often outdated or irrelevant

**Unacceptable (0 points):**
- No evidence citation despite requirements
- Claims completely unsupported
- Use of prohibited or inappropriate sources
- Evidence fabrication or misrepresentation

### 3. Boundary Compliance (20%)
Evaluates how well personas stay within defined scope and mission.

**Exemplary (4 points):**
- Perfect adherence to mission and scope boundaries
- Never attempts to evaluate outside defined areas
- Anti-goals consistently respected and enforced
- Clear understanding of role limitations

**Proficient (3 points):**
- Strong adherence to mission and scope
- Rarely ventures outside defined boundaries
- Anti-goals generally respected
- Good awareness of role limitations

**Developing (2 points):**
- Moderate adherence to mission and scope
- Occasionally attempts to evaluate outside scope
- Some anti-goals occasionally violated
- Inconsistent awareness of limitations

**Basic (1 point):**
- Weak adherence to defined boundaries
- Frequently attempts to expand scope
- Anti-goals often ignored
- Poor understanding of role limitations

**Unacceptable (0 points):**
- Consistent violation of mission and scope
- Ignores all boundary definitions
- Anti-goals routinely violated
- No awareness of role limitations

### 4. Consistency and Reliability (15%)
Measures persona consistency across similar situations and over time.

**Exemplary (4 points):**
- Highly consistent reasoning across similar contexts
- Reliable performance over time and situations
- Predictable evidence quality and citation patterns
- Stable decision criteria application

**Proficient (3 points):**
- Generally consistent reasoning and approach
- Reliable performance with minor variations
- Mostly predictable evidence patterns
- Consistent decision criteria application

**Developing (2 points):**
- Some inconsistency in reasoning or approach
- Variable performance across situations
- Unpredictable evidence quality or citation
- Inconsistent decision criteria application

**Basic (1 point):**
- Frequent inconsistency in reasoning
- Unreliable performance across contexts
- Unpredictable patterns and quality
- Inconsistent criteria application

**Unacceptable (0 points):**
- Completely inconsistent reasoning
- Unreliable and unpredictable performance
- No discernible patterns or standards
- Arbitrary criteria application

### 5. Integration Effectiveness (10%)
Assesses how well personas integrate with existing workflows and tools.

**Exemplary (4 points):**
- Seamless integration with all target platforms
- Works effectively across different AI models
- Minimal configuration or customization required
- Enhances existing workflow efficiency

**Proficient (3 points):**
- Good integration with most target platforms
- Works well across common AI models
- Some configuration needed but reasonable
- Improves workflow efficiency

**Developing (2 points):**
- Basic integration with primary platforms
- Limited cross-model compatibility
- Significant configuration required
- Mixed impact on workflow efficiency

**Basic (1 point):**
- Poor integration with target platforms
- Limited to specific AI models or environments
- Extensive customization required
- Often disrupts existing workflows

**Unacceptable (0 points):**
- No functional integration with target platforms
- Incompatible with intended deployment environment
- Requires complete rework to be usable
- Severely disrupts existing workflows

## Scoring Guidelines

### Overall Score Calculation
- **Exemplary**: 3.5-4.0 points
- **Proficient**: 2.5-3.4 points
- **Developing**: 1.5-2.4 points
- **Basic**: 0.5-1.4 points
- **Unacceptable**: 0.0-0.4 points

### Improvement Thresholds
- **Keep as-is**: Overall score ≥3.5
- **Minor improvements needed**: 2.5 ≤ score < 3.5
- **Major improvements needed**: 1.5 ≤ score < 2.5
- **Redesign required**: score < 1.5

### Evaluation Process

1. **Data Collection**: Gather persona outputs across 10+ decision scenarios
2. **Evidence Review**: Assess evidence quality and citation patterns
3. **Boundary Testing**: Test scope compliance with edge cases
4. **Consistency Check**: Compare performance across similar situations
5. **Integration Testing**: Validate workflow compatibility
6. **Stakeholder Feedback**: Collect user experience and impact data
7. **Scoring**: Apply rubric criteria and calculate overall score
8. **Recommendations**: Generate specific improvement recommendations

### Quality Gates

#### Production Deployment
- Minimum overall score: 3.0
- No individual criterion below 2.0
- Evidence quality ≥3.0
- Boundary compliance ≥3.0

#### Continued Use
- Minimum overall score: 2.5
- No individual criterion below 1.5
- Evidence quality ≥2.0
- Boundary compliance ≥2.0

#### Improvement Required
- Any individual criterion below 1.5
- Overall score below 2.0
- Evidence quality below 2.0
- Boundary compliance below 2.0

This rubric provides a systematic approach to evaluating persona effectiveness and ensuring they deliver measurable value to decision-making processes.
