# Enterprise Onboarding Orchestrator

Comprehensive enterprise onboarding automation system that orchestrates personalized learning paths,
manages knowledge transfer, and ensures successful team member integration with continuous progress
tracking and success metrics.

## Onboarding Orchestration Process

1. **Role Analysis**: Analyze team member role, skills, and learning requirements
2. **Path Generation**: Create personalized onboarding learning paths and workflows
3. **Knowledge Transfer**: Manage structured knowledge transfer from existing team members
4. **Progress Tracking**: Monitor onboarding progress and identify intervention points
5. **Success Validation**: Validate onboarding completion and team integration success

## Enterprise Onboarding Framework

### Role-Based Onboarding

- **Technical Roles**: Developer, DevOps, Security, Architecture onboarding paths
- **Product Roles**: Product Manager, Designer, Analyst onboarding workflows
- **Leadership Roles**: Team Lead, Manager, Executive onboarding programs
- **Support Roles**: Operations, Support, Compliance onboarding processes

### Learning Path Types

- **Technical Onboarding**: Codebase, tools, systems, and technical processes
- **Process Onboarding**: Workflows, methodologies, and team procedures
- **Culture Onboarding**: Team values, communication patterns, and collaboration
- **Domain Onboarding**: Business domain knowledge and industry context

## Onboarding Workflow Automation

### Role Analysis

```bash
# Analyze team member role and requirements
/enterprise-onboarding-orchestrator --analyze-role --member john.doe --role senior-developer

# Assess current skills and gaps
/enterprise-onboarding-orchestrator --assess-skills --member jane.smith --format detailed

# Generate learning requirements
/enterprise-onboarding-orchestrator --generate-requirements --role product-manager --team frontend
```

### Learning Path Generation

```bash
# Create personalized learning path
/enterprise-onboarding-orchestrator --create-path --member new.hire --duration 30-days

# Generate onboarding schedule
/enterprise-onboarding-orchestrator --schedule-onboarding --member team.lead --start-date 2025-01-15

# Setup learning milestones
/enterprise-onboarding-orchestrator --setup-milestones --path technical-onboarding --milestones 5
```

### Knowledge Transfer Management

```bash
# Assign knowledge transfer mentors
/enterprise-onboarding-orchestrator --assign-mentor --member new.dev --mentor senior.dev

# Schedule knowledge transfer sessions
/enterprise-onboarding-orchestrator --schedule-transfer --topic codebase --duration 2-hours

# Track knowledge transfer progress
/enterprise-onboarding-orchestrator --track-transfer --member new.hire --progress detailed
```

## Progress Tracking and Analytics

### Onboarding Metrics

- **Completion Rates**: Percentage of onboarding tasks and milestones completed

### Success Indicators

- **Technical Competency**: Ability to work independently on technical tasks
- **Process Integration**: Understanding and following team workflows and procedures
- **Team Integration**: Effective collaboration and communication with team members
- **Domain Understanding**: Grasp of business context and product knowledge

### Intervention Points

- **Skill Gaps**: Identify areas requiring additional training or support
- **Process Issues**: Detect difficulties with workflows or procedures
- **Integration Challenges**: Recognize social or collaboration challenges
- **Knowledge Gaps**: Find missing domain or technical knowledge
