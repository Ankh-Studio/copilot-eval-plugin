# Template Evaluation Rubric

## Structure (Weight: 0.25)

**Score 4**: Well-organized with clear sections, placeholders, and documentation. Easy to understand
and customize. **Score 3**: Good structure with minor organizational issues that don't significantly
impact usability. **Score 2**: Moderately structured but with confusing organization or missing
sections. **Score 1**: Poorly structured making it difficult to use or modify. **Score 0**: No clear
structure or organization.

## Flexibility (Weight: 0.25)

**Score 4**: Highly configurable with clear customization points and optional sections. **Score 3**:
Good flexibility with reasonable customization options. **Score 2**: Limited flexibility, requires
significant modification for different use cases. **Score 1**: Too rigid for practical adaptation.
**Score 0**: No flexibility, single-use only.

## Documentation (Weight: 0.20)

**Score 4**: Comprehensive documentation with usage examples, customization guides, and best
practices. **Score 3**: Good documentation covering most usage scenarios. **Score 2**: Basic
documentation missing important details or examples. **Score 1**: Minimal documentation that hampers
effective use. **Score 0**: No documentation provided.

## Best Practices (Weight: 0.15)

**Score 4**: Follows industry best practices and modern patterns with proper error handling. **Score
3**: Good practices with minor oversights. **Score 2**: Some practices that may lead to maintenance
issues. **Score 1**: Poor practices that could cause problems. **Score 0**: Ignores best practices
entirely.

## Reusability (Weight: 0.15)

**Score 4**: Designed for broad reuse across different projects and contexts. **Score 3**: Good
reusability with minor adjustments needed for different contexts. **Score 2**: Limited reusability,
requires significant changes for different projects. **Score 1**: Too specific for practical reuse.
**Score 0**: Single-use template not designed for reuse.
