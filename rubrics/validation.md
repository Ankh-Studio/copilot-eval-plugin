# Validation Rule Evaluation Rubric

## Coverage (Weight: 0.25)

**Score 4**: Comprehensive coverage of all important validation scenarios and edge cases. **Score
3**: Good coverage with most critical validation scenarios addressed. **Score 2**: Basic coverage
missing important validation scenarios. **Score 1**: Poor coverage with significant gaps in
validation logic. **Score 0**: No meaningful validation coverage.

## Accuracy (Weight: 0.25)

**Score 4**: Highly accurate validation with minimal false positives/negatives. **Score 3**: Good
accuracy with acceptable error rates. **Score 2**: Basic accuracy with notable false
positives/negatives. **Score 1**: Poor accuracy that may miss issues or flag correct code. **Score
0**: Inaccurate validation that cannot be trusted.

## Performance (Weight: 0.20)

**Score 4**: Fast validation with minimal impact on development workflow. **Score 3**: Good
performance with reasonable validation times. **Score 2**: Basic performance that may slow down
development. **Score 1**: Poor performance that significantly impacts workflow. **Score 0**:
Validation performance is unacceptable.

## Actionability (Weight: 0.15)

**Score 4**: Clear, actionable feedback with specific guidance for fixes. **Score 3**: Good feedback
with reasonable guidance for resolution. **Score 2**: Basic feedback that may require additional
interpretation. **Score 1**: Poor feedback that is difficult to act upon. **Score 0**: No actionable
feedback provided.

## Integration (Weight: 0.15)

**Score 4**: Seamlessly integrates with existing tools and workflows. **Score 3**: Good integration
with minor compatibility issues. **Score 2**: Basic integration requiring workarounds or manual
steps. **Score 1**: Poor integration that disrupts existing workflows. **Score 0**: No consideration
for integration.
