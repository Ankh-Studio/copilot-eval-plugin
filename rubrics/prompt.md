# Prompt Evaluation Rubric

## Clarity (Weight: 0.25)

**Score 4**: Unambiguous, specific, and easily understood by both humans and AI. Clear scope and expected output format.
**Score 3**: Generally clear with minor ambiguities that don't significantly impact results.
**Score 2**: Some unclear elements that may lead to inconsistent AI responses.
**Score 1**: Vague or confusing language that hampers effective AI assistance.
**Score 0**: Completely unclear or contradictory.

## Context (Weight: 0.20)

**Score 4**: Provides comprehensive relevant context, constraints, and background information.
**Score 3**: Good context with most essential information included.
**Score 2**: Basic context provided but missing key details.
**Score 1**: Minimal context that limits AI understanding.
**Score 0**: No relevant context provided.

## Specificity (Weight: 0.20)

**Score 4**: Precise requirements, clear constraints, and detailed specifications for expected output.
**Score 3**: Specific enough for consistent results with minor gaps.
**Score 2**: Moderately specific but allows too much interpretation.
**Score 1**: Overly general with insufficient guidance.
**Score 0**: No specificity or actionable guidance.

## Actionability (Weight: 0.20)

**Score 4**: Directly leads to concrete, implementable AI responses with clear next steps.
**Score 3**: Enables actionable outputs with minimal clarification needed.
**Score 2**: Produces somewhat actionable results requiring additional user guidance.
**Score 1**: Results are difficult to act upon without significant rework.
**Score 0**: Does not lead to actionable outcomes.

## Reusability (Weight: 0.15)

**Score 4**: Highly reusable across similar scenarios with minimal modification.
**Score 3**: Good reusability with minor adjustments for different contexts.
**Score 2**: Limited reusability, requires significant changes for reuse.
**Score 1**: Too specific for practical reuse.
**Score 0**: Single-use only, not designed for reuse.
