# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

We commit to fostering a safe and positive environment by:

### Expected Behavior

- **Be respectful** and considerate in all interactions
- **Use welcoming and inclusive language** 
- **Be collaborative** and value different perspectives
- **Gracefully accept constructive feedback** 
- **Focus on what is best for the community** 
- **Show empathy** towards other community members

### Unacceptable Behavior

- **Harassment** in any form (sexual, racial, gender-based, etc.)
- **Trolling** or deliberately disrupting discussions
- **Personal attacks** or insulting language
- **Publishing private information** without consent
- **Spam** or unsolicited promotional content
- **Threats** of violence or intimidation
- **Discrimination** based on any protected characteristic

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to all community spaces, including:
- **GitHub repositories** (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- **Email communications** related to the project
- **Public events** and meetups
- **Social media** channels associated with the project
- **Private communications** when relevant to community participation

## Enforcement

### Reporting Guidelines

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Contact the maintainers** directly at matt@ankhstudio.com
2. **Include details** of the incident (time, location, description)
3. **Provide evidence** if available (screenshots, logs, etc.)
4. **Request confidentiality** if needed

### Enforcement Process

1. **Initial Review** - Maintainer assesses the report within 48 hours
2. **Investigation** - Gathering facts from all parties involved
3. **Decision** - Determining appropriate action based on severity
4. **Resolution** - Implementing consequences and monitoring
5. **Follow-up** - Ensuring the resolution is effective

### Consequences

Consequences for violating the code of conduct may include:
- **Warning** - Official notice about unacceptable behavior
- **Temporary suspension** - Limited access to community spaces
- **Permanent ban** - Complete removal from community participation
- **Content removal** - Deleting offensive or harmful content
- **Mandatory education** - Required training on community guidelines
